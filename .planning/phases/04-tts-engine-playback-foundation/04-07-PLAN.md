## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-04-07-02 | Denial of Service | Rapid preview taps spawning unbounded synth jobs | mitigate | Preview button disabled during in-flight synth; cache hit on second tap; preview queue limited to one active synth. |
| T-04-07-03 | Information Disclosure | Per-book voice preference leaking reader identity | accept | Voice preference is config, not PII. Stays local. |
| T-04-07-04 | Tampering | Speed value outside safe range | mitigate | effectiveSpeedProvider clamps to [0.75, 2.0]; playback_bar speed picker only exposes 5 discrete values. |
</threat_model>
